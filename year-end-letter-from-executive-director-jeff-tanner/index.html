Year-End Letter from Executive Director, Jeff Tanner

Dear Friends,

What an incredible year! In early 2014, we set out to achieve an ambitious short-term vision. By the end of 2015, we would: 1) launch a startup incubator, and 2) help birth three Creation-restoring movements. Now, as we close out 2015 and get ready for the new year, we can reflect back on how God orchestrated and blessed our plans, and as we do so, you can celebrate alongside us, because…

We achieved our vision!

And we want to show our gratitude, because without such a strong missional community of advisors, impact investors, friends, and family who came alongside us (and our first batch of startup founders), we would not be where we are today. But, this is only the beginning.

On January 8, 2016 (it’s almost upon us) the Launch Phase for our next startup group–batch two–begins. I’m impressed and inspired by the missional passion, capabilities, and ideas of the founders who will join us, and I can’t wait to see how God uses them as they launch difference-making ideas into viable, scalable organizations.

As with batch one, we can’t do this without you. Will you please consider supporting this important work by donating to Wayfare Labs as part of your year-end giving?

With your support, we’ve:

  • Created and piloted the Launch Phase of our startup program, helping seven founders birth four missional organizations.

  • Begun pursuing our long-term vision: making Costa Mesa and Orange County known for missional startups like the Bay Area is known for tech startups.

  • Developed an advisor network of over 30 founders and experts who are generously giving time and energy to impart wisdom and experience to our startups.

  • Celebrated our first launch night, where over 100 impact investors, advisors, family members, and friends heard pitches from our batch one startups.

  • Begun piloting the Growth phase of our program, offering the practical, emotional, and spiritual guidance to help these early stage organizations expand their impact.

  • Tested and refined our own business model and program structure, resulting in a completely revamped and more impactful experience.

We are grateful for the community gathering around this vision and fueling this work. Together, we are not only launching new movements that restore what is broken and create beauty in our world, we are also developing the next generation of creators, founders, and adventurers for the common good.

In 2016, we plan to birth ten creation restoring organizations, support ten more in their growth stage, and successfully transition at least one to sustainable autonomy.

As we come to the end of this year, will you consider funding the next stage of this vision? Every contribution helps, now matter how big or small. Donate online at www.wayfarelabs.org, or send a check to the address in this letter’s footer.

For the world’s best future,

Jeff Tanner Executive Director Wayfare Labs
