Share Your World-Changing Idea

Do you have a world-changing idea (even if it only solves a small problem)? Tell us about it! By filling out the form below, you will enter our Wayfare Labs “World-Changing Ideas Contest.” Don’t worry, we’ll keep your idea top secret if you want (just let us know on the form below). Feel free to submit ideas that are still just dreams. At Wayfare Labs, we love ideas and want to support the people who share them. The best idea will be eligible to win a surprise prize package from Wayfare Labs.

There are two ways to submit your idea. Either fill out this form, or submit a video! If you’re looking to submit a video, follow these instructions:

  • Post your video online (Facebook, YouTube, etc.).
  • Your video should answer the following questions:
    • Tell us the problem(s) your idea solves. What’s wrong with the world that needs to change and why?
    • Tell us about your solution (Your Idea!) and how it solves the problem(s) you articulated earlier.
    • How will your idea change the world?
  • Fill out a couple details on this form (give us the URL of where your video is hosted in the form field below).
  • Hit submit! If you agree to let us share your idea with others, we may even post your video on our social networks!
