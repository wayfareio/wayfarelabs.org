Communications Internship Opportunities

Wayfare – 270 Baker St East, Suite 200, Costa Mesa, CA - wayfare.io

To apply, please send cover letter and resume to elisha@wayfarelabs.org

Wayfare exists to unite and propel creators, founders, and adventurers for the common good. Wayfare is a coworking space where over 70 members are all working on their own businesses in shared space with one another. We pride our self on a culture of creativity, collaboration, and care for each other. Wayfare Labs is the non-profit arm of the Wayfare brand. The interns would play a part and gain experience in both for-profit and non-profit work.

Job Brief

We are looking for people who are passionate about pursuing the words best future through the integration with their work and life. If you’re a student looking to work alongside individuals creating and adventuring for the common good then we’d like to get to know you and discuss more about this opportunity.

  1. Social Media Internship

    *Plan and execute social media campaigns
    *Coordinate with Graphic Designer to create assets
    *Work with various social media accounts and create copy and content for specific audiences

  2. Copywriter Internship

    *Curate content for blog
    *Contribute as a writer for various blog postings
    *Create content for email marketing
    *Assist with curriculum and content development for various programs

  3. Graphic Designer Internship

    *Create graphic assets for social media marketing, email marketing, and event promotional materials

  4. Fundraising Internship

    *Assist ED of non-profit with setting up fundraising opportunities
    *Create fundraising campaigns and generate ideas for effective fundraising

  5. Founding Project Internship

    *Assist with the initial set up of a non-profit organization
    *Conduct research on various topics to create content for a future product
    *Gain first-hand experience of starting an organization alongside Wayfare founder
